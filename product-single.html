Our raw wildflower honey is harvested in small batches right here on the farm. Because we never pasteurize or ultra-filter our honey, it retains all the subtle floral notes, natural pollen, and beneficial enzymes.

Perfect for drizzling over warm biscuits, stirring into tea, or enjoying right off the spoon.

  • 100% Raw, Unfiltered Wildflower Honey
  • No additives, preservatives, or artificial syrups.

Note: Raw honey will naturally crystallize over time. Place the jar in warm water to reliquefy. Do not feed to infants under 1 year of age.
